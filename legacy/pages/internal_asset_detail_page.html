Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum investment amount?

The minimum investment for this property is $100, making it accessible to a wide range of investors. You can invest in increments of $50 above the minimum.

How are returns distributed?

Dividends are distributed quarterly based on the property's net operating income. Distributions are made automatically to your BuildVest wallet, which you can then withdraw to your bank account or reinvest.

Can I sell my shares before the hold period ends?

There is a 90-day minimum holding period. After that, you can list your shares on BuildVest's secondary marketplace where other investors can purchase them. Please note that secondary market prices may differ from your purchase price.

What are the tax implications?

You will receive a K-1 tax form annually for your share of the property's income and deductions. Income from commercial real estate can qualify for various tax benefits including depreciation deductions. Please consult with a tax professional regarding your specific situation.

What happens if the property doesn't perform as expected?

All investments carry risk. The property is professionally managed and has strong fundamentals, but returns are not guaranteed. We maintain reserve funds for unexpected expenses and have insurance coverage in place. Investors receive regular updates on property performance.

Who manages the property?

The property is managed by Urban Developments, a professional property management firm with over 15 years of experience managing commercial real estate. They handle day-to-day operations, tenant relations, and maintenance.
