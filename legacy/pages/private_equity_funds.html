Private equity funds, venture capital funds, real estate investment funds, hedge funds, infrastructure funds, and alternative investment funds can all be tokenized. Both new fund launches and existing portfolios are supported.

Tokenization doesn't change your fund's legal structure or governance. Tokens represent economic interests in the fund through SPV structures. You maintain control over investment decisions while smart contracts handle distributions and investor relations.
