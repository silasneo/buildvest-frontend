Platform Security

BuildVest implements multiple layers of security:

  • 256-bit SSL Encryption - All data transmitted through our platform is encrypted
  • Cold Storage Wallets - The majority of digital assets are stored offline
  • Multi-Factor Authentication - Required for all account access and transactions
  • Segregated Accounts - Your funds are held separately from company operating capital
  • Regular Security Audits - Third-party penetration testing and vulnerability assessments
  • KYC/AML Compliance - Rigorous identity verification for all users

We also maintain comprehensive insurance coverage and work with leading cybersecurity firms to ensure the highest level of protection.

Two-factor authentication (2FA) is mandatory for all BuildVest accounts. To set it up:

  1. Go to Account Settings → Security
  2. Click "Enable Two-Factor Authentication"
  3. Download an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
  4. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app
  5. Enter the 6-digit code to confirm setup
  6. Save your backup codes in a secure location

You'll need to enter a code from your authenticator app whenever you sign in or make transactions. Keep your backup codes safe in case you lose access to your authenticator device.

If you suspect unauthorized account access:

  1. Immediately change your password from Account Settings → Security
  2. Review your recent activity log for suspicious transactions
  3. Contact our security team at security@buildvest.com or through live chat
  4. We will temporarily freeze your account while investigating
  5. Enable additional security measures as recommended by our team

BuildVest monitors all accounts for suspicious activity 24/7. You'll receive email alerts for any login attempts from new devices or unusual transaction patterns.

Account Management

Creating a BuildVest account is simple and takes about 5 minutes:

  1. Click "Start Investing" or "Sign Up" on our homepage
  2. Enter your email address and create a secure password
  3. Verify your email through the confirmation link
  4. Complete your profile with personal information
  5. Complete KYC verification by submitting government-issued ID
  6. Link a payment method (bank account or crypto wallet)
  7. Set up two-factor authentication

Once your KYC verification is approved (typically within 24 hours), you can start browsing and investing in opportunities.

BuildVest accepts multiple payment methods for maximum convenience:

  • USDC (Stablecoin) - Instant processing, no fees
  • Bank Transfer (ACH) - 1-3 business days, no fees for deposits over $500
  • Wire Transfer - Same day processing, $25 fee
  • Credit/Debit Cards - Instant processing, 2.9% processing fee

For withdrawals, you can receive funds via:

  • Bank transfer (ACH) - Free, 1-3 business days
  • USDC to your wallet - Free, instant
  • Wire transfer - $25 fee, same day

To update your account information:

  1. Log in to your account
  2. Navigate to Account Settings → Profile
  3. Update the information you need to change
  4. For sensitive changes (address, name), you may need to re-verify your identity
  5. Click "Save Changes"

Note: Changes to banking information or crypto wallet addresses require additional verification for security purposes and may take 24-48 hours to process.

Each individual is limited to one personal account. However, you can create additional accounts for:

  • Business Entity - Register your company as a separate investor
  • Trust Account - Invest on behalf of a trust (requires legal documentation)
  • IRA/Retirement Account - Self-directed retirement investing (coming soon)

Each account type requires separate KYC verification and appropriate documentation. Contact our support team for guidance on setting up entity or trust accounts.

Legal & Compliance

Yes, BuildVest operates under full regulatory compliance:

  • SEC Registration - Registered as a broker-dealer and investment platform
  • FINRA Member - Subject to FINRA oversight and regulations
  • KYC/AML Compliance - Full compliance with Bank Secrecy Act requirements
  • Regular Audits - Annual financial audits and regulatory examinations

All investment offerings on BuildVest are structured to comply with Regulation D, Regulation A+, or Regulation Crowdfunding under securities laws.

Investment returns and capital gains on BuildVest may be subject to various taxes:

  • Income Tax - Distribution payments are typically taxed as ordinary income
  • Capital Gains - Profits from selling shares may be subject to capital gains tax
  • 1099 Forms - We provide all necessary tax documents by January 31st each year

Tax treatment varies based on:

  • Your individual tax situation
  • Type of investment (real estate may have additional considerations)
  • Holding period (short-term vs. long-term capital gains)
  • Account type (individual, entity, or retirement account)

We recommend consulting with a tax professional for advice specific to your situation. BuildVest does not provide tax advice.

BuildVest is currently available to:

  • U.S. Residents - Must be 18+ and have a valid SSN or ITIN
  • Accredited Investors - Access to all investment opportunities
  • Non-Accredited Investors - Access to Regulation A+ and Regulation Crowdfunding offerings
  • Entities - Corporations, LLCs, partnerships, and trusts with proper documentation

International investors: We are working to expand to additional countries. Join our waitlist to be notified when BuildVest becomes available in your region.

Accredited Investor Definition: Individual with $200k+ annual income ($300k+ joint), $1M+ net worth (excluding primary residence), or certain professional credentials.

  • Operational Risk - Property management or project execution issues

Important considerations:

  • Past performance does not guarantee future results
  • Projected returns are estimates, not guarantees
  • Diversification can help manage but not eliminate risk
  • Only invest what you can afford to lose

Technical Support

  5. Try different browser - Test in an incognito/private window

Transaction processing times vary by payment method:
